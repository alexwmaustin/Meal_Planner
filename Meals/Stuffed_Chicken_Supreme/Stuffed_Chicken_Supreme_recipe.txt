Season outside of chicken with salt & pepper, put aside until ready to stuff
Combine diced bread, basil, diced tomatoes, & feta in a bowl, sprinkle with olive oil
Season with salt and pepper
Divide Filling into 2 oz portions
Cut Breasts down the middle and stuff filing into them
Brush chicken with olive oil and place in roasting pan
cover with aluminum foil and roast in oven for 20min at 400
remove foil and roast for another 10min
